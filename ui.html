5 Tips to stay Healthy & Fit

Here are few tips suggested by the doctors that will help you to remain in shape even when you are not training

BALANCED DIET

Eat a variety of foods including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Aim for portion control and try to limit processed foods and sugary drinks.

Learn More

ADEQUATE SLEEP

Prioritize getting 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Good sleep is crucial for overall health and maintaining energy levels.

Learn More

STAY HYDRATED

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Proper hydration supports digestion, circulation, and helps keep your skin healthy.

Learn More

MANAGE STRESS

Practice stress-reducing techniques such as meditation, deep breathing, yoga, or engaging in hobbies you enjoy. Chronic stress can impact both your mental and physical health.

Learn More

REGULAR EXERCISE

Incorporate both cardiovascular exercises and strength training into your routine. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.

Learn More
